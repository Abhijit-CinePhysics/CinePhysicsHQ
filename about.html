Where Physics Meets Visualization

What is CinePhysics?

CinePhysics is an educational platform dedicated to making Physics visual, interactive, and accessible.

Through simulations, virtual laboratories, worksheets, question banks, and educational content, CinePhysics helps students move beyond memorization toward genuine conceptual understanding.
